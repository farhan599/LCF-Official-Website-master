About Us

In Balochistan, groundwater budgets are poorly understood. In conjunction with physiological complexity, excessive pumping is exacerbating the problem. Climate change, complex terrain, varying hydrogeology, water management issues combined with data scarcity amplify the modeling and monitoring challenges. Physical modeling and manual monitoring are practically not possible, especially accounting for the unregulated pumping. With the recent development of the Internet of things (IoT) enabled smart systems, automated remote monitoring with sensors is gaining popularity. In Pakistan (including our Sindh projects), such sensors are being used for water resources management. In the proposed study, data acquired from automated sensors will be used for modeling that will help in metering the aquifers with reliable estimates.
